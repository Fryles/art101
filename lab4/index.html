    Pseudocode of Baking Focaccia

  • Prepare the Dough:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, olive oil, and yeast.
    • Stir until the dough turns sticky.
  • First Rise:
    • Put your dough in a lightly (olive) oiled bowl and cover it with plastic wrap or a towel.
    • Let it rise in a warm area for about 1-2 hours.
  • Shape the Focaccia:
    • Preheat your oven to 425 degrees.
    • Drizzle a 9x13 baking pan with some olive oil.
    • Gently deflate the dough and transfer it to the prepared 9x13.
    • Press and stretch the dough to fill the pan, creating dimples with your fingers.
  • Second Rise:
    • Cover your dough with plastic wrap or a towel.
    • Let it rise for about 20 minutes while the oven is heating.
  • Toppings:
    • Brush the top of the focaccia with more olive oil.
    • Add your choice of toppings, such as rosemary, thyme, olives, cherry tomatoes, onions, garlic, and sea salt.
  • Bake:
    • Put your focaccia in the oven and bake for 25 minutes or until golden brown.
